About

Learn Quran Academy

Learn Quran Academy is a Non-Partisan organization, not part of any group or mosque. We welcome all students interested in learning Quran. Our small team includes project managers, engineers, and tutors guided by experienced Ustadhs to provide Online Quran Learning services for Muslim brothers and sisters worldwide.

We recognize that many people are engaged in full-time work and family life. Therefore, we offer one-to-one live interactive classes online for learning the Holy Quran using Skype and Zoom, ensuring high-quality communication and teaching.

Our Core Goals

  • Innovating the Quran Learning Methods
  • Teaching Quran in an interactive way
  • Building Moral character of our students
  • Enable everyone learn Quran

Our teaching method is very successful and our all students are very pleased with our service. Therefore, we are offering Free Trial Classes in which you can simply evaluate our services.

Who are we?

Learn Quran Academy was established in 2006 with the aim of teaching the Holy Quran from Arabic alphabets to reading Quran with Tajweed in U.K and all over the world. Our key aim in mind is to prepare children who are unable to read Quran with Tajweed and for adults also who can read but not aware of Tajweed rules.

Our Services
Our Mission

Learn Quran Academy aims to serve and assist such Muslims either adults or children by providing online one-to-one live interactive classes for learning the holy Quran and Islamic education at the most suited time to them. We want to spread the teachings of Quran to the Muslims living all around the world.
